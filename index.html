About Us

Welcome To GRJIS

We prepare school students for life, helping them develop an informed curiosity and a lasting passion for learning. We are committed to developing your child’s potential for life in a changing world. An overall development of 3H (Head, Heart and Hand) through excellent Academics, Sports and Games, other Co-Curricular activities and serving the community in the best possible manner. To provide a learning culture to the children where they can attain a behavior and self realization power like a Sage (Rishi) and Qualities and Knowledge like a King (Raja), thus turning into Royal Rajrishi (Raja+rishi). We believe in “Culture of Religion and Knowledge of Ethics” which makes a distinctive contribution in molding a child of 21st century promoting spiritual, moral, cultural, mental and physical development of child and society. We also prepare our child for opportunities, responsibilities and experiences of later life.
